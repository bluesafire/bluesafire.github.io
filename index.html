Bansuri (Indian Bamboo Flute)

The Bansuri is a melodious wind instrument used in Indian classical and folk music. It has a deep connection with Indian spirituality and culture.

Tabla

The Tabla is a pair of drums used in Indian classical music. It provides rhythm and is essential for accompaniment in traditional performances.

Tanpura

The Tanpura is a stringed instrument that provides a continuous harmonic drone in Indian classical music, setting the tonal atmosphere for performances.
