Class 2/24 – Sa Re Ga

✅ How to hold the Bansuri
✅ Blowing pressure & soft blowing technique
✅ Singing & playing the first three notes – Sa, Re, Ga

Practice:

  • 🧘‍♂️ 5 min meditation
  • Blowing pressure: Soft & steady (5 min)
  • Singing notes: Sa, Re, Ga (10 min)
  • Playing each note separately (10 times each)
  • Playing Sa Re Ga smoothly (10 min)

Class 3/24 – Sa Re Ga Ma

✅ Singing & playing four notes – Sa, Re, Ga, Ma

Practice Plan:

  • 🧘‍♂️ 5 min meditation
  • Singing (10x each + phrases in reverse)
  • Playing individual notes and phrases
  • Line Practice (Sing & Play 5x):
    • Sa
    • Sa Re Sa
    • Sa Re Ga Re Sa
    • Sa Re Ga Ma Ga Re Sa

Class 4/24 – Skip & Range Alankara

✅ 5-sec blowing pressure practice
✅ Playing Skip & Range Alankara

  • 🧘‍♂️ 5 min meditation
  • Sing + Play Skip Alankaras (Sa Ga Re Ma …)
  • Range Alankara: Sa–Re, Sa–-Ga, Sa–––Ma
  • 💡 Keep blowing steady, adjust pitch by covering holes
